The concept

Room investors can buy room nights in-advance without telling the dates.

Room investors can resell the room nights after 1 year or stay if room is available.

Room renters get 5 USD worth of tokenroom# for every room night of stay.

Room renters can sell tokenroom# after the value of their aggregate tokenroom# has increased more than 60USD.
